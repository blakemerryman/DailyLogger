Daily Logger Help
-----------------

List of options:

(no options)  : Opens today's log in your prefered text editor.
--help   | -h : Prints out this document to the console for reference.
--list   | -l : Lists today's log to the console.
--append | -a : Appends the trailing arguments to today's log as its own entry. Please escape !-marks. Prefer to enclose entry in double quotes ("...").
